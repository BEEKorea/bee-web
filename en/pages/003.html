The purpose of BEE is to assist the national Church in the establishment of continuing, indigenous Church-based training centers in which servant-leaders are equipped to develop growing and reproducing churches.

First,

Ministry by Nationals

The national church knows how to train their people in their context. Hence BEE establishes indigenized BEE ministry in a nation by translating BEE materials into their languages, training national facilitators and administrators, and financial independence.

Second,

Ministry in the Church

Church leaders can be trained best under the supervision and train from experienced church leaders. Hence BEE trains church leaders for their practical ministry in the church.

Third,

Inter-denomination Ministry

BEE trains new pastors and church leaders regardless of their denomination. The students from various denominations who completed layered BEE training programs will work together in a church-based training center.

Fourth,

Train as Church Leaders

BEE trains the students to be church leaders who are equipped with biblical knowledge, holy characteristics and ministry skill so that they can be recognized by other church leaders.

Fifth,

Growth

BEE trains church leaders to transform their churches as healthy growing churches in quality and quantity by equipping the saints to be involved in God-given ministry and by planting new churches.

Sixth,

Discipleship Through Spiritual Multiplication

The most important purpose of the church is to make disciples through spiritual multiplication. There were devoted disciples such as the Twelve to Jesus, Joshua to Moses, Elisha to Elijah, and Timothy to Paul. Hence BEE makes disciples through spiritual multiplication.

Seventh,

Demand-based Ministry

BEE works in the countries where demand trained devoted church leaders including Islamic, Hinduist, Buddhist, and communist countries.
