BEE takes three biblical principles as our ministry philosophy.

First,

Evangelism and Discipleship

BEE emphasizes evangelism and discipleship most because they are crucial to obey the Great Commission of the Lord.

Second,

Total Training

The total training of BEE includes the following:

  • Biblical knowledge (What to know): BEE provide the solid word of God from the workbook of each course.
  • Ministry skill (What to do): All trained church leaders must be equipped with not only biblical knowledge but also ministry skills to be involved in practical ministry.
  • Christian character (What to be): Church leaders need to develop the Christian character in 1 Timothy 3.

Third,

Transferability

BEE training is designed to multiply church leaders effectively through a specific strategy and method to train large number of church leaders.
