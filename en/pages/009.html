HQ Missionary

They Are

Missionaries devoted to BEE ministry fully belonging to the HQ

Qualification

  • Facilitator (If married both)
  • Complete OSOM (training for a long-term missionary), TP (training for short-term missionary) or equivalent
  • Complete BMT (BEE Missionary Training)
  • Facilitated at least 3 BEE courses including One-to-One Discipleship Traing and Galatians

Responsibility

50 aCHs/year

Application

Contact BEE Korea

Branch Missionary

They Are

National missionaries belonging to a branch

Qualification

  • Facilitator
  • Complete the training for a branch missionary
  • Produce 1 group of graduates (at least 8 students) of DPM

Responsibility

50 aCHs/year

Application

Contact BEE Korea
