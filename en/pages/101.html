Outline

It is an one-year program offering twice a year (last weeks of Feb and Aug). It includes opening and graduation services and retreat. Galatians for 7 weeks and Romans for 8 weeks follow an opening service. There will be a break during which the students either take special seminars or participate in an outreach. The students will study Christian Life after the break and a graduation service will follow.

Objectives

  1. Know how to take a nonbeliever to Christ and be involved in personal evangelism.
  2. Train new believers with One-to-One Discipleship Training to equip them in Christ.
  3. Understand the Gospel clearly that one can be saved by the faith in Jesus Christ.
  4. Explain the law and grace clearly and know how to apply it to your life and ministry.
  5. Start your spiritual training that will be continued during your life including regular Bible reading, prayer and fellowship with other believers.
  6. Know the basic Bible study method and study the Bible regularly using the method.
  7. Understand the way to harmonize your marriage and family life according to the principles from the Bible, and develop a plan to improve your marriage and family life and take actions.
