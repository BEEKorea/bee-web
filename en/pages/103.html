Outline

It is designed to equip Christian workers. They will learn the content and power of the true Gospel, and study the theological base and its application to grow spiritually after their salvation. Also it helps them become Jesus Christ's disciples and prepare them to make disciples for Jesus Christ.

Objectives

  1. Explain God’s redemptive plan through the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  2. Follow Jesus as His disciples.
  3. Use life-style evangelism to save the lost.
  4. Make disciples for Jesus.
