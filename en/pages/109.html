Program Outline

This program mainly targets the students between 10-14 years old. This 8-volum series have 6 lessons in one volume to provide the biblical fundamentals of Christian true faith and life. The program uses small group discussions so that the students can think and make conclusions by themselves about any doubt and question that they may have at their level of understanding. The program includes a special feature of Group Activity which is designed to guide the students to find valuable biblical lessons through diverse interesting activities.

  • It takes six 90-minute sessions to complete one volume.

Contents

  1. 8-volume series RIJ includes questions, small group discussions and Group Activities for the students which guide their self-study. Each volume has 6 lessons and it takes 90 minutes to complete a lesson.
  2. The titles of the 8 volumes are; 1. Really? Can I Be a Christian? 2. Really? Can I Have Faith? 3. Really? Can I Understand the Bible? 4. Really? Can I Pray? 5. Really? Can I Be a Disciple? 6. Really? Can I Discern the World? 7. Really? Can I Win? 8. Really? Can I Have a Vision?
  3. The students will learn about Jesus Christ, true faith, and the foundation of faith which is necessary to live as Christians in the world.

Prerequisite

  • None
