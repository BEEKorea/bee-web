Overview

It is the first volume of this series and an introduction to all consecutive series including the church, faith, the Bible, prayer and the Holy Spirit.

  • It takes six 90-minute sessions (total 9 hours) to complete the course.

Contents

  1. This course has 6 lessons. You will study what the church is, how important faith is, how the Bible is base of your faith and what kind of relationship you have with God.
  2. This course is not a lecture but to encourage students to think for themselves and share their thoughts with other students.
  3. You might promote your learning by participating Group Activity and doing your assignment.

Prerequisite

  • None
