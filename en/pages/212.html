Overview

Defind the importance of prayer, what the prayer is, how you could pray and practice praying while you understand the reason and method of praying.

  • It takes six 90-minute sessions (total 9 hours) to complete the course.

Contents

  1. You pray seriously when you have urgent crisis. Find the examples and results of overcoming crisis with prayer.
  2. Learn what the true prayer is with the good prayer models and Jesus Christ.
  3. In the center of the prayer there are thanks for God.
  4. Practice praying everyday by praying with other students.
