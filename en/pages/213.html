Overview

To be a Christian means to be a Jesus' disciple. Jesus trained his disciples. Confirm the way to be a disciple and what you suppose to do in your life as a true disciple.

  • It takes six 90-minute sessions (total 9 hours) to complete the course.

Contents

  1. Check whom Jesus calls and how, various ways of the training.
  2. You can't be his disciple at once. Ubderstand the process of being mature through training and obeying even though they were very weak in the beginning.
  3. The disciples follow Jesus and they should follow Jesus' cross too.
  4. The disciples should spread out to evangelize.
