Overview

Everybody has a vision. Define Christians' visions and understand the beginning point of those visions.

  • It takes six 90-minute sessions (total 9 hours) to complete the course.

Contents

  1. Define the true vision and find out somebody's new life because of the vision.
  2. A vision is not a simple hope nor desire. Find out the origin of the true vision and the way to find it.
  3. Define what you suppose to do in the future and what you should prepare for it. Apply it in your life.

Prerequisite

  • Really 1, 2
