Vision Statement

Equip church leaders in the world with BEE program to reproduce dedicated Christian workers according to the Great Commission of Jesus Christ.

History

Stage 1: Pioneering Stage(1993-2002)

  • 1993, introduced BEE to Onnuri Community Church, Korea
  • 1994, Inauguration service
  • 1994, Pioneered Chinese ministry
  • 1997, Pioneered Vietnamese ministry
  • 1999, Pioneered Nepali and Sri Lankan ministry
  • 1999, Established South Asia Branch
  • 2000, Pioneered Qatari ministry
  • 2000, Pioneered Saudi ministry

Stage 2: Expanding Stage(2003-2012)

  • 2003, Began Onnuri BEE Academy (OBA)
  • 2005, Established Middle East Branch
  • 2005, Registered BEE Korea as an incorporated mission agency
  • 2006, Established Central Asia and China Branches
  • 2008, Established America and Africa Branches
  • 2012, Divided Africa Branch into East Africa Branch and West Africa Branch
  • 2012, Began Onnuri Bible Club (OBC)

Stage 3: Indigenizing Stage(2013-2022)

  • 2013, Completed BEE Center
  • 2014, 20th Anniversary BEE International Conference
  • 2015, Starting Developing REALLY?! Series
  • 2016, Established Word of Life International Christian College (WLICC) under Word of Life Mission International, a non-profit organization registered in Illinois, USA
  • 2016, Recruiting 1st students for WLICC (Nepal, China)
  • 2017, BEE Korea Conference (Qatar)
  • 2018, Completed 8 volumes of REALLY?! Series
  • 2018, Established and registered BEE India
  • 2018, Established BEE China
  • 2019, WLICC begins in E Africa
  • 2019, WLICC the First Graduation (Nepal)
